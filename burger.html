Make your Own Burger

Ingredients

  1. Buns
  2. Lettuce
  3. Tomatoes
  4. Pickle
  5. Cheese
  6. Burger Sauce

Recipe

  1. Gently burn the top of both buns.
  2. Add burger sauce to the top and the bottom buns.
  3. Add Lettuce, tomatoes, pickle, and cheese.
  4. Lock them together tightly with both the buns.
  5. Gently heat the buns until the cheese inside melts.
  6. Sprinkle chilli flakes on top for added heat.
